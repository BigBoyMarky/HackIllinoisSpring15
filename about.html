About Us

Get tired of clicking though pages? Would you like to see all of your or your friends' social media accounts in one place? Yeah, we agree, it's tiring having to check Facebook, Twitter, and Google+ every day, which is why we created SocialUnion.

Who are we? We're a group of 4 college students from Georgia Tech, Miami University, and Purdue who met at HackIllinois 2015 to make this lovely web application.
